# Customer Segmentation Insights

## Overview

Customer segmentation groups users based on behavioral patterns to better understand different types of platform users.

Segmentation was performed using user-level activity metrics such as:

- product views
- cart activity
- purchases
- session frequency

## Key Segments Identified

The analysis identified several behavioral user groups:

• **Browsers**
Users who primarily view products but rarely add items to cart or complete purchases.

• **Cart Users**
Users who frequently add products to cart but do not always convert to purchase.

• **Purchasers**
Users who complete transactions and represent direct revenue contributors.

• **Low Engagement Users**
Users with minimal interaction or single-event activity.

## Business Interpretation

Different user segments require different strategies:

- Browsers may need stronger product engagement.
- Cart users may need incentives to complete checkout.
- Purchasers represent the most valuable customers and should be retained.

## Recommendations

• Offer **targeted promotions** to cart users to encourage purchase completion.

• Implement **remarketing campaigns** for browsers.

• Provide **loyalty rewards or personalized offers** to high-value purchasers.

• Use segmentation to drive **personalized marketing strategies**.
